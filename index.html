Biography

Hewan is a final-year B.Tech. student in Computer Science at Madanapalle Institute of Technology and Science (MITS). He is currently working as a research intern in the Lab of Software and Service Engineering at Innopolis University under the supervision of Ms. Swati Megha. He received his High School Diploma degree with first class from National Examination Board, Nepal in 2018.

Hewan’s research interests revolve around Machine Learning and Computer Vision. He is currently working on medical image segmentation and analysis. He is also a member of Universal Association of Computer and Electronics Engineers (UACEE), International Association of Engineers (IAENG) and Association for Information Science and Technology (ASIS&T).
