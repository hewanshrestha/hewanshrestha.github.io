I am a graduate student pursuing Masters in Visual Computing at Saarland University, Germany. Currently, I am doing my Master Thesis under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Dietrich Klakow and Dr. Usman Naseem. Along with my studies at Saarland University, I am currently working as a HiWi (Research Assistant) in the Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing Group (I2SC) led by Prof. Dr. Ingmar Weber (Alexander von Humboldt Professor for AI).

I received my Bachelors degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur, India. I completed my Bachelors thesis in collaboration with Innopolis University, Russia, where I was supervised by Prof. Dr. Manuel Mazzara.
