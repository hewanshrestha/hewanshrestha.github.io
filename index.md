I am a second-year graduate student pursuing <a href="http://www.master-visual-computing.de/">Masters in Visual Computing</a> at <a href="https://uni-saarland.de/en/">Saarland University</a>, Germany. Along with my studies at Saarland University, I am currently working as a HiWi (<em>Research Assistant</em>) in the <a href="https://www.i2sc.net/">Interdisciplinary Institute for Societal Computing Group (I2SC)</a> led by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=3YDUbP0AAAAJ">Prof. Dr. Ingmar Weber (Alexander von Humboldt Professor for AI)</a>.

I received my Bachelors degree in Computer Science and Engineering from <a href="https://jntua.ac.in">Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur</a>, India. I completed my Bachelors thesis in collaboration with <a href="https://innopolis.university/en/">Innopolis University</a>, Russia, where I was supervised by <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=th6g2bgAAAAJ">Prof. Dr. Manuel Mazzara</a>.
